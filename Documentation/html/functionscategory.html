DiscreteWavelets Toolbox    

Functions — By Category

Audio List included audio files and their attributes, and play wavelet vector transformations.
Denoising Estimate noise, compute thresholds, test for sparseness, and perform denoising.
Filters Haar filters, Daubechies filters, Coiflets, biorthogonal spline filters, CDF97, and the LeGall filter.
Fourier Tools Cosine and Sine in terms of complex exponentials, generate of finite Fourier series, and trigonometric polynomials.
Graphics Get image information; plot images, wavelet transforms, and Huffman trees.
Image Processing Perform color space conversions, compress images, and apply gamma correction and histogram equalization to images. Generate Huffman codes for input strings or images.
Measurements Compute cumulative energy, entropy, mean square error, and PSNR. Perform basic image compression.
Miscellaneous Resize vectors, manipulate "corners" of matrices, get information about data files that come with the package, manipulate 3-D arrays, determine the maximum number of iterations that can be performed on a vector or matrix, manipulate wavelet transformations.
Transformations Compute one- and two-dimensional wavelet transformations. Included are the Haar transformation, orthogonal wavelet transformations, biorthogonal wavelet transformations, and transformations via the lifting scheme.
