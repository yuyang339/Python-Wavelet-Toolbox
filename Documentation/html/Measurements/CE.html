CE

Cumulative Energy

Syntax

	y = CE(v)

Description

y=CE(v) takes as input a matrix or vector v and returns the cumulative energy vector associated with v.

Example

Create a vector and plot it and its cumulative energy: 

x=0:.001:.999; %Sample points
v=sin(2*pi*x); %Create a vector
plot(v); %Plot the vector
y=CE(v); %Compute the cumulative energy
plot(y); %Plot the cumulative energy

results in

Now compute a wavelet transformation of v and plot it and its cumulative energy: 

wt=WT1D1(v,Daub(6)); %Compute the wavelet tranformation of v
WaveletVectorPlot(wt,1); %Plot the wavelet transformation
w=CE(wt); %Compute the cumulative energy of wt
plot(w): %Plot the cumulative energy
