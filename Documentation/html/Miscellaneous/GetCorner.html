GetCorner

Select a corner of a matrix

Syntax

	B = GetCorner(A,n,m)

Description

B = GetCorner(A,n,m) takes as input a matrix A and positive integers m and n and returns the m x n submatrix that is the upper left-hand corner of A.

Remarks

The integers m and n must be less than or equal to the numbers of rows and columns of A, respectively.

B=GetCorner(A,m,n) is equivalent to the command B=A(1:m,1:n).

Example

A=round(10*rand(8,8)) %Create a matrix
B=GetCorner(A,4,3) %Get the upper left 4 x 3 corner of A


A =

     3     9     4     9     8     6     6     7
     8     6     1     9     4     1     7     2
     4     5     2     5     2     2     6     4
     9     1     1     5     4     4     5     6
     2     9     2     3     1     8     5     8
     3     6     2     9     1     0     3     1
     1     4     4     4     9     0     7     9
     1     5     0     1    10     2     2     8


B =

     3     9     4
     8     6     1
     4     5     2
     9     1     1
