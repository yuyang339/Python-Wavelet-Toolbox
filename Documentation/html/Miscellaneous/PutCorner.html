PutCorner

Select a corner of a matrix

Syntax

	C = PutCorner(A,B)

Description

C = PutCorner(A,B) takes as input two matrices A and B and returns a matrix where the upper left-hand corner of A has been replaced by B.

Remarks

The dimensions of B must be less than or equal to the dimensions of A.

Example

A=round(10*rand(8,8)) %Create a matrix
B=zeros(3,4); %Create a 3x4 zero matrix
C=PutCorner(A,B) %Replace the upper left 3x4 corner of A with B.


A =

     9     8     4     4     6     2     2     1
    10     2     8     6     5     6     9    10
     5     9     6     1     0     3     2     0
     1     3     5     1     3     7     8     8
     1     2     9     5     2     7     5     8
     3     3     3     8     8     7    10     9
     8     6     8     9     3     5     1     1
     3     5     8     1     5     1     4     4


C =

     0     0     0     0     6     2     2     1
     0     0     0     0     5     6     9    10
     0     0     0     0     0     3     2     0
     1     3     5     1     3     7     8     8
     1     2     9     5     2     7     5     8
     3     3     3     8     8     7    10     9
     8     6     8     9     3     5     1     1
     3     5     8     1     5     1     4     4
